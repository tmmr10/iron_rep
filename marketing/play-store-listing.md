# Play Store Listing — IronRep

## English

### App Title (max 30 chars)
IronRep — Workout Tracker

### Short Description (max 80 chars)
Track your workouts, beat your PRs, and build your perfect training plan.

### Full Description (max 4000 chars)
IronRep is a clean, focused workout tracker built for people who actually lift. No social feeds. No AI coaches. Just your workouts, your progress, and a simple way to get stronger.

TRACK EVERY SET
Log weight, reps, and set type for every exercise. IronRep remembers your last performance so you know exactly what to beat. Guided mode walks you through each exercise — or switch to list mode for a quick overview.

BUILD YOUR PLAN
Create custom training plans with 50+ exercises across every muscle group. Drag to reorder, set target sets, and start your workout with one tap. Share plans with friends via a simple link.

SEE YOUR PROGRESS
Watch your strength grow over time with clear charts for every exercise. Track personal records, total volume, workout streaks, and muscle group distribution — all in one place.

NEVER MISS A WORKOUT
The activity calendar shows your training history at a glance. Weekly summaries keep you accountable. Every workout is saved with full details — weights, reps, duration.

YOUR DATA STAYS YOURS
All data is stored locally on your device. No account required. No cloud sync. No tracking. Just open the app and train.

FEATURES
• Guided workout mode with rest timer
• 50+ exercises with muscle group targeting
• Custom training plans with drag-and-drop editor
• Strength development charts per exercise
• Personal records tracking (weight, reps, volume)
• Workout history with calendar view
• Weekly streak tracking
• Plan sharing via links
• Dark and light theme
• German and English language support
• Remove ads with a one-time purchase

Built for lifters who want a simple, reliable workout log — not another fitness social network.
